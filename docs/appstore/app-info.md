## App 描述

### 繁體中文

**推廣文字（170 字以內）：**
> 回到 1812 年的國會殿堂！在這款融合卡牌策略與政治辯論的遊戲中，你將扮演一名議員，透過出牌、辯論與投票來影響議案走向。建立你的勢力，運用策略擊敗對手，成為國會中最有影響力的政治家！

**描述：**
> 《1812 國會風雲》是一款結合卡牌策略與社交推理的多人對戰遊戲。
>
> 在 19 世紀初的國會中，每位玩家扮演不同派系的議員，透過打出攻擊牌、防禦牌、策略牌來影響議案的投票結果。
>
> 🃏 核心玩法：
> • 收集並組合強力卡牌
> • 在辯論階段發揮口才與策略
> • 透過投票機制決定議案成敗
> • 建立同盟或背叛盟友
>
> ⚔️ 遊戲特色：
> • 獨特的「辯論」出牌機制
> • 多種議員角色與專屬能力
> • 即時多人對戰
> • 排行榜與成就系統
> • 定期更新的擴充卡牌包

### English

**Promotional Text (170 chars max):**
> Step into the parliament of 1812! Play cards, debate, and vote your way to political dominance in this unique blend of card strategy and social deduction.

**Description:**
> Parliament 1812 is a multiplayer card strategy game set in the political arena of the early 19th century.
>
> Each player takes on the role of a parliament member from different factions, using attack, defense, and strategy cards to influence the outcome of legislative votes.
>
> 🃏 Core Gameplay:
> • Collect and combine powerful cards
> • Use strategy and rhetoric in debate phases
> • Vote to determine the fate of bills
> • Form alliances—or betray them
>
> ⚔️ Features:
> • Unique "debate" card-play mechanics
> • Multiple parliament member roles with special abilities
> • Real-time multiplayer battles
> • Leaderboards and achievements
> • Regular content updates with expansion packs
